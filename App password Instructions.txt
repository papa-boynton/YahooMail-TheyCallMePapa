App Password in Yahoo

1)Sign in to your Yahoo Account Security page. (https://login.yahoo.com/account/security?.lang=en-US&.intl=us&.src=yhelp)
2)Under "External connections," click Create app password.
3)Enter your app's name in the text field. (ie:Droptop YahooMail app)
4)Click Generate password.
5)Use the one-time password to log in to your 3rd party app.
4)Click Done
